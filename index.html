The problem

Clients arrive informed
about everything except their options.

Clients are researching treatments before they ever book — watching videos, comparing before-and-afters, reading about Botox, filler, Sculptra, PRF, RF microneedling, and skin tightening. But many still arrive at consultations unsure of what treatment is right for them, what results are realistic, and what questions to ask.

Providers are left trying to educate, correct expectations, explain anatomy, discuss cost, and build trust in a limited consultation window.

Clients come in asking for

  • realistic result previews
  • treatment comparisons
  • cost transparency
  • maintenance planning
  • help knowing what to prioritize first

Providers spend time on

  • correcting unrealistic expectations
  • explaining anatomy from scratch
  • justifying pricing
  • building trust in a short window
  • re-explaining the same basics

Our approach

Built for natural results.

This tool is not designed to create beauty-filter transformations, exaggerated lips, overfilled cheeks, or unrealistic jawlines. The goal is to support natural facial balancing, prevention-focused aging management, and transparent cost conversations — so clients feel informed, not pressured.

Not a diagnostic tool. Does not replace an in-person evaluation by a qualified provider. Designed to support better conversations between clients and professionals.

Founder story

I built the tool
I needed to find.

"Black don't crack — but I still reached a point where I felt like I was cracking. Not dramatically. In that quiet, personal way where you start noticing small shifts. Before trying any treatment, I wanted to understand what was realistic. I wanted to compare outcomes before walking into a medspa and trying to explain my goals from scratch."

What I found were beauty filters. Tools that changed the face too much. Results that looked too perfect. None of them felt like they were designed with Black women, natural facial balancing, or realistic aging prevention in mind.

I did not want a fantasy face. I wanted a thoughtful preview — something that could help me ask better questions and walk into a consultation feeling informed instead of overwhelmed.

So I built it.

— Tamara Richards, Founder
